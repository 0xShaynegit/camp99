Purpose of Camp 99

Camp 99, named after our street address,
is serving the ’99’, the Flock of the Lord!

 
We envisage Camp 99 to be a place where Christian groups, both foreigner and Thai, can hold meetings, Bible studies, prayer and worship times, sports events, concerts, picnics and more.
/ 02

services

Serving the 99

events

Indoor meeting room is available 7:30am until 10pm.  Outdoor recreation and game area is available 8am until 6pm. Soccer/footballs, volleyballs, badminton equipment and ping-pong items are provided. We even have pullover practice jerseys for football! Outdoor concerts are possible if you provide extra lighting and sound equipment. There’s a campfire pit near the pond that’s got a nice feel, even if it is a touch warm out!

Meetings/Worship/Prayer/Small Groups

The inside meeting room 50sq.m. with two sofas, two long tables, and 20 chairs. Has 55″ smart TV with fiber optic internet, DVD player, and powerful, clear speakers. Acoustic-electric guitar (but no amp yet) and drum box are available for you to use. Water cooler and cups are in this room.
 
Our beautiful pond has a sala/cabana for up to 8-12 people. We also have a 3mx3m tent/awning if needed. Full kitchen is available to use, however we are unable to provide meals at this time.

fishing

There are indeed Talapia fish in the pond which we stock every couple of months. We have three spinning rods and seven bamboo poles. Some supplies are provided in our tackle box.

Sports

Footsal field can handle 6v6 adults or 8v8 kids. It is 42×22 meters. Sturdy volleyball net is on grass and is regulation height for women; lined with chalk.  Regulation Takraw court with durable net… can double as badminton since they’re the same exact dimensions.  Also have a fun badminton area, not lined yet. Covered ping-pong area is in the car-park. A second table is available if reserved in advance.

COME ON OUT!

We're just 8km from the Super Highway
Camp 99 is basically my home (Robert Bowling) on five rai that I am offering to be used by churches, dorms, Bible schools, foundations, etc. to give them a ‘peaceful, country setting’ for meetings and/or recreation.  There is no charge and it’s not a public venue; it’s simply me opening up my home to the brothers and sisters in Christ. Contact me about a one-time event or a recurring one. We will have a calendar of events on here soon!
 
NOTE: With the Covid-19 situation we require masks inside, but they’re optional outdoors.
